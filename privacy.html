Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our AI thumbnail generation service:

  • Video descriptions and prompts you provide
  • Images you upload for thumbnail generation
  • Text overlays you specify
  • Payment information (processed securely by PayPal/Stripe)
  • Usage data (number of credits, generation history)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Generate AI thumbnails based on your specifications
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Track your credit usage and plan status
  • Improve our AI models and service quality

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and we implement appropriate security measures:

  • All data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage)
  • No personal data is stored on our servers
  • Payment processing is handled by secure third-party providers
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For thumbnail generation
  • PayPal: For payment processing
  • Stripe: For payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally in your browser)
  • Delete your data (clear your browser's localStorage)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request information about data processing
